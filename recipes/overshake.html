Crazy milkshake recipe

The "Tangy Berry Bliss" is a one-of-a-kind extreme milkshake that combines the luscious creaminess of strawberry ice cream with the zesty tang of sour marmalade. Prepare yourself for a flavor explosion like no other!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of strawberry ice cream
  • 1/4 cup of sour marmalade
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • Whipped cream, for topping
  • Fresh strawberries, for garnish (optional)
  • Sprinkle of rainbow sprinkles (optional)

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine the strawberry ice cream, sour marmalade, and whole milk.
  2. Blend on high speed until all the ingredients are thoroughly mixed and the shake is smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour the milkshake into a large glass or mason jar, leaving a little space at the top for the toppings.
  4. Add a generous swirl of whipped cream on top of the milkshake.
  5. Finish by garnishing with fresh strawberries, and if you want an extra touch of fun, add a sprinkle of rainbow sprinkles.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy the marvelous combination of sweet and tangy flavors in every sip.

Note: Feel free to adjust the ingredient quantities according to your personal preference and desired consistency.
